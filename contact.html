Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EventHub

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our events page, click on an event you're interested in, and click the "Register" button. You'll need to create an account first if you haven't already.

Can I cancel my event registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the event starts. Go to your account dashboard and manage your registrations there.

How do I create an event as an organizer?

Sign up for an organizer account, then access the admin dashboard where you can create and manage your events with our easy-to-use tools.

Are there any fees for using EventHub?

EventHub is free for attendees. Event organizers pay a small service fee per ticket sold, which helps us maintain and improve the platform.

What if I need technical support during an event?

We provide 24/7 technical support during live events. You can reach us through the contact form or our emergency support line.

Still have questions?

Our support team is standing by to help you with any questions or concerns you may have.
